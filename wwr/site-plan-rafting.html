Overview

Purpose

The purpose of the site is let audience to get know the activities that the rafting company brings to people. It includes a navigation site over company’s activities that brings. It must to inform to the people about activities, ages, dates, costs. And It have to be visually attractive as well.

Audience

The main audience is people from 12 to 50 years old. People that usually seeks over internet to plan funny activities or vacations. These people usually go to similar activities with their families. They seek for a having a good time in a beautiful place, with rememberable experiences with an according price for entire family or friends. The site is expected to be easy to find the information that they are looking for. This site will be used mostly by a smart phone, and a laptop as well.
